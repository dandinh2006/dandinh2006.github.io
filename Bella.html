    A little background about Bella

    Bella is two years and four months old. came from a somewhat immature and poisonous family. They delivered her here to the bunny cafe after deciding they didn't want to keep her and dumped her right away. We don't want her to be an unwanted bunny, so we've been working on finding her a home.

    a little bit about bella's personality!

    She adores being around relatives and cuddling. She is somewhat timid and dislikes having her actions dictated to her. She only respects her family and possibly close friends, and she detests being around visitors.
